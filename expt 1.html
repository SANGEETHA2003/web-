KERALA

Kerala, located in the southwestern region of India, is often referred to as “God’s Own Country” due to its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. This state is known for its lush forests, pristine beaches, and serene backwaters. The state is also famous for its traditional dance form, Kathakali, and Ayurvedic medicine.
